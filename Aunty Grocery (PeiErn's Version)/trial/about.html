About Us

Aunty Grocey has been open 20 years in the market which expand around Malaysia.In early years, Aunty Grocery was a small business locted at Taman Maluri near the Sunway Velocity.After 3 years, they expand 5 grocery shop then later expand 200 grocery around the Malaysia. Right now, Aunty grocery just started open e-commerce to offer a services to customer. To let customer go to Aunty Grocery website to buy grocery.


Lim Ah Weng is the founder of Aunty Grocery. He graduated from Beijing University in 1997 and came back to Malaysia to find job around in KL. After 3 months, he could't fint a job. So, he work with his mom in supermarket as a cashier.


In 2000, he resigned from his job and create his own business which is grocery. During that time, he has not enough money so he rent a small shop-lot to do his business in Taman Maluri. After 3 years, he expand his business which open 5 grocery shop. After that, he open more and more...
